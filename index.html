Synopsis

Lee Carthur Nery, a talented guitarist studying at Bicol University Polangui, gets discovered by Marky and Keyt. Despite his success, he never forgets his roots and continues to inspire the next generation of musicians. This is the story of how one man's passion for music changed his life forever.
